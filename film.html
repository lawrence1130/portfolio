Short Film • 2024

Behind The Lens.

A visual exploration of urban isolation and the neon-lit streets of the city. A passion project bridging cinematography and storytelling.

Directing Color Grading Editing

The Process

"Behind The Lens" was born out of a desire to experiment with low-light cinematography and aggressive color grading. I wanted to capture the feeling of being the only person awake in a city of millions, focusing on the interplay of practical neon lighting and shadow.

With zero budget and just a borrowed mirrorless camera, I spent three weeks shooting between 2 AM and 5 AM to ensure completely empty streets. The real magic happened in post-production, where I handled the entire editing, grading, and sound design pipeline.

Production Toolkit

  • DaVinci Resolve (Color Grading)
  • Premiere Pro (Non-linear Editing)
  • Ableton Live (Soundscape Design)
